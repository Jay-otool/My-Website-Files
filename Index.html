Why Mattel Is Evil!

I used to be a big Thomas the Tank Engine fan when I was a kid. Yes, I grew up with CGI Thomas (please forgive me, model series fans). Looking back, the show was brain-dead and catered to five-year-olds like me at the time.

That’s because after Thomas and the Magic Railroad messed up the series, Britt had to sell to HIT Entertainment. The series then took a mindless baby show direction with all intelligence sucked out of it. Eventually, HIT was bought by Mattel, and they turned it into CGI and, later, a god-awful, cursed 2D animation.

Fuck you, Mattel.
